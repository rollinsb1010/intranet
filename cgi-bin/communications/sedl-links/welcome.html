SEDL Links

A bimonthly newsletter, SEDL Links informs SEDL staff, board members, legislators, and selected "friends of SEDL" about SEDL's new staff, products, fieldwork, and upcoming events. SEDL Links is being redesigned and will become quarterly starting with the Spring 2005 issue.For more information, e-mail the editor of SEDL Links, Debbie Ritenour at dritenou@sedl.org or call her at ext. 276.
